**Building digital tools for the common good.**

DigitalCommons Coop is a cooperative organisation dedicated to creating open source software that serves the common good. We build tech to power movements for social and ecological justice. We believe in the power of collaboration, shared ownership, and building technology that works for everyone.

## ü§ù About Us

We're a community of developers, designers, storytellers and organisers working together under cooperative principles. Our projects are built in the open, for the open, with input and contributions from people around the UK.

### Our Values

- **Open by Default** - We believe in transparency and open source as the foundation for trustworthy technology
- **Democratic Governance** - Every contributor has a voice in our direction
- **Shared Ownership** - We build together, we benefit together
- **Community First** - Technology should serve people and communities, not the other way around

We welcome contributors of all skill levels and backgrounds! Here's how you can participate:
